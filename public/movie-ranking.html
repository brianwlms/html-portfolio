BDubs Best Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time

Braveheart

One of the greatest movies of a reluctant hero that inspires a nation to overcome overwhelming odds.

Memento

This movie uses time to disorient the viewer to both emphasize the protagonist's struggle, and creates a sense of tension to unfold the story.

Shawshank Redemption

A profound story of accepting your circumstance, but not falling victim to it.
The hero overcomes his wrongful conviction and makes small progress to freedom. In a world where everyone around him accepts their fate, Andy never gives up hope and continues to push towards freedom.
